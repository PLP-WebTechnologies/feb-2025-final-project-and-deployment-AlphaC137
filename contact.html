Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? ‚ñº

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn and unwashed items with original tags attached. Returns are free for orders within the US. For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

How long does shipping take? ‚ñº

Domestic orders (US) typically take 3-5 business days to arrive. International shipping can take anywhere from 7-14 business days, depending on the destination and customs processing times.

Do you ship internationally? ‚ñº

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see the specific shipping options available to your country during checkout.

How can I track my order? ‚ñº

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the carrier's site.
