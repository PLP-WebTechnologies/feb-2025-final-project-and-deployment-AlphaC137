Our Story

What started as a school project for the Power Learn Project Scholarship has turned into something much bigger! Originally, this website was created as part of a learning assignment, but the more I worked on it, the more I realized the potential. Combining the functionality of an e-commerce site with the engaging features of a blog seemed like the perfect blend.
